Inception

Inception - 2015

In 2015, Computer Science student at the time Felix Abreu (Founder of BlueiT) made a trip to his motherland Dominican Republic with the goal of reconnecting with his family. Having visited different parts of the country he noticed a consistent "missing piece" in many small business establishments, modern day technology. 

Majority of the businesses Felix visited sold their products using a simple calculator to compute their daily transactions. Noticing this "missing piece" led Felix to wonder, how much more successful could these businesses be if they had access to technological services that would help them achieve their highest potential?

Establishment

Establishment - 2019

Implementation of business oriented technology is one of the many irrefutable factors that account for the continued success of businesses all around. Staying up to date with current technologies is vital to ensuring businesses stay one step ahead of their competitors. With technology constantly every-changing, how can businesses stay up to date with the tools they need in order to stay ahead in the game?

Building Leaders Up Everywhere in Technology (BlueiT) was established with the goals of providing technological services to businesses to help them achieve their highest potential and to provide mentorship to individuals looking to become a part of the tech industry.
